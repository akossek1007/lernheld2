# LernHeld Curriculum & Pedagogy Guide

This document describes how LernHeld implements the NRW primary school curriculum (Grundlehrplan NRW) using structured pedagogical methods.

## 1. Mathematics: The "Kernaufgaben" Approach

In accordance with PIKAS, LernHeld focuses on "Kernaufgaben" (core tasks) as the foundation for multiplication.

### 1x1 Learning Path (9-Week Progression)

| Phase | Week | Focus | Pedagogical Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1** | 1-3 | 10er-Reihe | Bündelungsprinzip (10er-Blöcke) |
| **Phase 2** | 4-6 | 2er-Reihe | Verdoppeln (Sockenpaare, Schuhe) |
| **Phase 3** | 7-9 | 5er-Reihe | "Kraft der 5" (Handbilder) |

### Addition & Subtraction (ZR 100)
- **Zehnerstopp**: Tasks like `48 + 7` are broken down into `48 + 2 = 50` and `50 + 5 = 55`.
- **Adaptive Engine**: Promotion happens at >85% accuracy, while demotion occurs at <60% to ensure consolidation.

## 2. German: FRESCH & Word Recognition

LernHeld supports the transition from phonetic spelling to orthographic correctness.

### NRW Vocabulary Integration
- **111 Merkwörter**: Frequently used but irregularly spelled words (e.g., *vielleicht*, *bisschen*).
- **50 Sichtwörter**: High-frequency functional words for "Blitzlesen" (flash reading).

### Exercise Types
- **Silbenschwingen**: Using highlighting to emphasize word structure.
- **Blitzlesen**: Training visual recognition for sight words.
- **Spaced Repetition**: Failed words are repeated with 3x probability to ensure mastery.
